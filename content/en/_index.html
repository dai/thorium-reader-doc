Thorium Reader is the ebook reader for Windows, MacOS and Linux.

This EDRLab application is in constant development and is now the reference for accessing EPUB 3 publications in reflow or fixed layout format, audiobooks and visual narratives, PDF documents and DAISY ebooks; LCP protected or not.

It is localized in a large set of languages, each version offering new locales.

Huge efforts are also made to get Thorium Reader highly accessible for visually impaired and dyslexic people.

This application is free, with no ads and no leaks of private data. This is the perfect tools for heavy readers, library patrons and students.

  • Improved text highlighting engine and several under the hood improvements (performance, accessibility, operating system integration, etc.)
  • Handling of diacritics in the "find text" / "search" feature
  • Better support for vertical writing mode and right-to-left (RTL) direction
  • New option to disable RTL inversion in the user interface
  • Adds Slovenian/Slovenščina and Danish/Dansk, update Portuguese (Portugal) and Swedish, fix bug with the variants of Chinese (traditional vs. simplified) and Portuguese (Portugal vs. Brazil)
