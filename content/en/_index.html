Enhance your reading experience with Thorium Reader, the ultimate desktop reading app! Looking to escape into a new novel, catch up on reading for class, or explore a new topic? Thorium Reader has got you covered. Completely free, with no ads or data leaks to worry about. Perfect for heavy readers, library-goers, and students alike. Developed by EDRLab, a non-profit dedicated to open source software and standards for the publishing industry, Thorium Reader is your go-to choice for a seamless, enjoyable reading experience. Try it now and see for yourself!
